Our Story

Christian worked overnight as a tech lead to support agents. One fateful night, December 22, 2014, a beautiful new woman in the office grabbed Christian's attention immediately. As he got to know Chimére, Christian realized that she was not only cute but smart, quirky and very loving. After she invited him to go to the Dominican Republic with a group of her friends, Christian decided to befriend Chimére and hang out with her every weekend; such has been the case since her birthday celebration in 2015. In fact, since then they've fallen in love and created spectacular memories together. Their magical adventures include attending beer festivals, quinching culinary cravings & bike pedaling in Seattle, pub hopping in Atlanta's L5P, hookah lounging in San Francisco, roller coaster riding in Orlando, slot machine exploration in Las Vegas and truly the list grows with each month.


They are life travel partners. They are lovers. They are very best friends.

Christian

Christian is usually toting his laptop and typing away at some bit of code. Otherwise, catch him if you can -- he'll be riding his bike, which is his favorite pastime, cooking up something decadent or sneaking away to watch his favorite anime. He is a pretty laid back guy who knows how to work hard to play harder.

Chimére

A creative professional, Chimére is likely hunting for vintage or antique furniture, painting / repurposing an interesting furniture piece or riding her bike, MRLY, on the bike lanes of Atlanta on any given weekend. She’s a certified unicorn trainer with a serious travel bug since she grew up in a military family. She delights in her curiosities and a good chat about branding and marketing.
