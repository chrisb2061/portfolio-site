Welcome

Club Ultra nightclub and ultra lounge is the newest upscale addition to the Atlanta nightlife scene.

Located at Piedmont Road and Lindbergh Drive in Buckhead and housed in the notorious former Gold Club building, Club Ultra nightclub and ultra lounge features sleek, modern design complimented by stylish ambience, an impressive DJ lineup, a one-of-a-kind champagne bar and an expansive signature cocktail menu.
