In The Club

Club Ultra nightclub and ultra lounge is the newest upscale addition to the Atlanta nightlife scene. Located at Piedmont Road and Lindbergh Drive in Buckhead and housed in the notorious former Gold Club building, Club Ultra nightclub and ultra lounge features sleek, modern design complimented by stylish ambience, an impressive DJ lineup, a one of a kind champagne bar and an expansive signature cocktail menu.

Club Ultra plays host to Atlanta's elite young and fashionable crowd, but leave the pretentiousness at the door. On any given night you can find yourself partying with the "who's who" of Atlanta's industry players, as well as a variety the entertainment industry's hot list. Once home to a dramatic adult nightlife scandal that captivated the nation, Club Ultra nightclub and ultra lounge has restored the dilapidated building - transforming it from a vacant eyesore into Atlanta's premier high-end nightclub venue and private event space that focuses on customer service and providing guests with an unforgettable social experience time and time again
