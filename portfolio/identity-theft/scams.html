Phishing Scams

Shopping online has become a norm for all. You can get almost anything online. You can buy your groceries, jewelry, clothes, furniture and much more all online. Internet buying makes everything more convenient for the buyer and prices are much lower than in big box stores. Internet thieves are now preying on these individuals who shop online. They use what is known as phishing. Phishing is is a way of attempting to acquire information such as usernames, passwords, credit card details and addresses by masquerading as a trustworthy entity in an electronic communication or transaction . Unsuspecting internet shoppers are tricked into giving their information in anticipation of receiving some sort of good or service.
